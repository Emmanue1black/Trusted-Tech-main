About Us

Phoenix CBT & Information Technology Centre is a JAMB UTME/DE–accredited registration center located in Ado-Ekiti. We are dedicated to providing top-notch services in computer-based testing (CBT), registration, and information technology solutions. Our center is equipped with modern facilities, reliable internet connectivity, and a conducive environment to ensure that every student, candidate, or client has a smooth and stress-free experience. Beyond JAMB registration, we also offer a wide range of ICT-related services, training programs, and technical support tailored to meet the needs of today’s digital age.

Our Mission is to empower students and the wider community by making access to education and technology seamless, reliable, and efficient. We aim to bridge the gap between learning and technology by providing innovative solutions that enhance academic success and digital literacy. At Phoenix CBT & IT Centre, we are committed to excellence, integrity, and continuous improvement, ensuring that every candidate who walks through our doors is equipped with the right tools and support to achieve their goals.
